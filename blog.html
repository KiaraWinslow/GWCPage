The Club

Founded 2017

Due to the amount of GWC Summer Immersion Program Alumni at ASTI, a group of students got togeather and decided to found ASTI's first GWC Club, dedicated to giving female students exposure to Computer Science and the Tech Space.

LinkedIn Feild Trip

LinkedIn Headquarters, San Francisico March, 2019

Because of a contact devoloped through the #BuiltByGirls program, the entire club was able to travel to LinkedIn headquarters in San Francisco on International Womens Day. There, we had the opportunity to have lunch with, and hear from a panel of women from diverse backgrounds in an assortment of positions.
